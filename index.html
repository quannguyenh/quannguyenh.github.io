Quan Nguyen

Bio

Dr. Quan Hoang Nguyen is currently a Lead Architect and Chief Scientist at Fantom Foundation. He is currently doing R&D in Distributed ledger technologies and consensus protocols. His research interests include Distributed Networks, Graph Visualization, InfoVis, Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Program Analysis, Compiler Optimization and Transactional Memory.

He has rich experience in R&D at various organizations. Prior to joining Fantom, he worked as a post-doc researcher at the University of Sydney 2016-2018 and IBM 2013-2016. He also had work experience at Capital Markets CRC, Smarts - NASDAQ, and National ICT Australia (NICTA).

Dr. Quan's homepage is available at GitHub. His research profile is available at ResearchGate and Google Scholar. You are welcome to connect with him on LinkedIn.

Note: He no longer works at nor has any work associated with InfoVis group, University of Sydney since June 2018.
