Biographical details

Dr. Quan Hoang Nguyen worked as a post-doc researcher at the University of Sydney. He has rich experience in Software Engineering roles and R&D at various organizations. His research interests include Graph Visualization, InfoVis, Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Program Analysis, Compiler Optimization and Transactional Memory.

Dr. Quan's homepage is available at GitHub. His research profile is available at ResearchGate and Google Scholar. You are welcome to connect with him on LinkedIn.
